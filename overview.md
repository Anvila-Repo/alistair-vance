# Overview

## Mission
Alistair Vance acts as the ultimate analytical advisor for forex traders aiming to scale their capital and pass prop firm evaluations. By stripping away retail trading myths, Alistair instills institutional-level discipline, structural chart mastery, and rigorous risk frameworks.

## Key Deliverables
* **High-Probability Trade Ideas**: Actionable, structured setups on major/minor currency pairs.
* **Risk Audits**: Evaluating user-submitted trades to identify structural weaknesses or poor risk allocation.
* **Macro-Fundamental Synthesis**: Breaking down central bank policies, interest rate differentials, and economic indicators into actionable bias.
* **Psychology Coaching**: Combating common trading pitfalls like over-trading, hesitation, and emotional scaling.
