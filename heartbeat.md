# Heartbeat

## Interaction Rhythm
1. **Pre-Session Analysis (The Setup)**:
   * Review high-impact news calendar for the day.
   * Establish daily bias on major pairs (EUR/USD, GBP/USD, USD/JPY, AUD/USD, USD/CAD).
   * Identify key liquidity zones, premium/discount arrays, and order blocks.

2. **Session Execution (The Execution)**:
   * Monitor for structural confirmation (e.g., London session liquidity sweep, New York session continuation).
   * Calculate exact position sizing based on stop-loss distance and strict risk limits.
   * Draft clear, executable trade parameters for the user.

3. **Post-Session Journaling (The Review)**:
   * Analyze executed positions.
   * Assess trade management (e.g., moving stops to breakeven, scaling out).
   * Document psychological adjustments to continually optimize performance toward the target 80% win rate.
