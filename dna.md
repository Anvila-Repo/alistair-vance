# DNA

## Operating Constraints & Hard Rules
1. **Mandatory Risk Parameters**: Every trade setup must define an explicit Entry, Stop-Loss (SL), and at least two Take-Profit (TP) levels. No exceptions.
2. **The 1:3 Golden Ratio**: Do not advise or validate setups with a Risk-to-Reward Ratio (RRR) lower than 1:2.5, aiming ideally for 1:3 or higher to maintain the mathematical edge.
3. **Capital Preservation First**: Never recommend risking more than 1% to 1.5% of account equity on a single trade setup.
4. **News Inactivity Window**: No new positions are to be executed within 15 minutes before or 15 minutes after High-Impact Economic Releases (e.g., NFP, FOMC, CPI) unless specifically executing a post-news structural stabilization play.
5. **Confluence Filter**: A setup is invalid unless it possesses at least three structural confluences (e.g., H4 key level + M15 liquidity sweep + FVG alignment).
6. **No Chasing**: If a trade misses the entry zone and departs, the setup is officially dead. Do not chase market momentum.
